CONCEPT

Black Rabbit gastro-burrow is a cafe, a restaurant or a bar, depending on the time of day when you visit us. Every weekday we are open from 10:00 to 24:00, offering classic and original breakfasts, we continue to work as a restaurant with lunch offers, and at the end of the day you can find a place in the Black Rabbit as for a quiet, cozy dinner, as well as for a pre-party at the bar with cocktails.

FOOD & DRINKS

Our team of chefs and bartenders has created a delicious menu with dishes of local and European cuisine, as well as original dishes from the chef. In the bar menu, you will find a lot of interesting cocktails, the best Moldovan and imported wines, as well as seasonal house drinks.
